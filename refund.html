Refund Policy

We strive for every order to be perfect. Please read our refund rules carefully — due to the instant and irreversible nature of digital goods, our refund terms are strict.

Effective: 1 January 2025 Last Updated: 1 June 2025

Core Refund Policy

NO REFUNDS AFTER SUCCESSFUL RECHARGE DELIVERY. Once an in-game recharge has been successfully delivered to the Player ID provided at checkout, the transaction is final and no refund will be issued under any circumstances.

This strict policy exists because digital goods — including diamonds, UC, gems, and other in-game currencies — are instantly consumed and cannot be recalled, reversed, or reclaimed once credited to a game account. This is consistent with standard digital goods industry practices and Indian consumer protection guidelines for digital services.

We strongly recommend all users double-check their Player ID and Server ID before confirming payment. A wrong ID means the recharge goes to another player's account and cannot be recovered.

When a Refund May Be Issued

While our general policy is no-refund after successful delivery, we recognise that legitimate exceptions exist. A refund may be considered in the following limited scenarios:

How to Request a Refund

If you believe your situation qualifies for a refund under the eligible scenarios above, follow these steps:

  • Step 1 — Contact us within 24 hours of the failed or erroneous transaction.
  • Step 2 — Email us at nemexisz999@gmail.com with the subject line: "Refund Request – [Your Order ID]"
  • Step 3 — Include the following in your email:
    • Your registered email address or mobile number
    • Your Order ID (found in your email confirmation)
    • Screenshot of the payment confirmation (from Razorpay or your bank)
    • Description of the issue with any relevant screenshots
  • Step 4 — Our support team will review your request and respond within 48 hours.

For faster support, you can also reach us on WhatsApp at +91 70999 74271. Share your Order ID and issue and our team will respond promptly.

Refund Processing

Approved refunds are processed back to the original payment method used for the transaction:

  • UPI / GPay / PhonePe: Refunded within 3–5 business days
  • Debit / Credit Card: Refunded within 5–7 business days (depending on your bank's processing time)
  • Net Banking: Refunded within 5–7 business days

Refunds are processed by Razorpay and NEMEXIS has no control over the exact time it takes for funds to appear in your account after approval. Bank processing times may vary.

We do not issue refunds in cash, NEMEXIS credits, or any method other than the original payment source. Refund requests submitted after 24 hours of the transaction may not be accepted.

Chargebacks & Disputes

Initiating a chargeback or payment dispute through your bank for a successfully delivered recharge order is a violation of our Terms of Service. By placing an order with NEMEXIS, you agree not to dispute any transaction where the digital goods were delivered as described.

In the event of a fraudulent or unwarranted chargeback, we reserve the right to:

  • Immediately suspend or permanently ban your NEMEXIS account
  • Provide Razorpay and your bank with full transaction evidence including delivery confirmation
  • Pursue recovery of the disputed amount through appropriate legal channels

If you have a genuine concern about a transaction, please contact us directly first. We are committed to fair resolution and will do everything in our power to address legitimate disputes promptly.

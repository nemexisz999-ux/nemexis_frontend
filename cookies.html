Effective: 1 January 2025 Last Updated: 1 June 2025

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, provide a personalised experience, and give website owners analytical information about how their site is used.

Cookies do not contain personally identifying information on their own, but they may be linked to personal data we hold about you (such as your logged-in account). Cookies cannot run programs or deliver viruses to your device.

NEMEXIS uses cookies to keep you signed in, remember your preferences, and help us understand how users navigate our platform so we can improve it. We do not use cookies to serve third-party advertisements.

When you visit NEMEXIS, certain third-party services may also set cookies on your device. These third parties have their own privacy and cookie policies, and we encourage you to review them:

Managing Cookies via Your Browser

Most browsers allow you to view, manage, delete, and block cookies through their settings. Here is how to access cookie settings in major browsers:

  • Google Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
  • Mozilla Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings → Privacy, Search, and Services → Cookies and site permissions

Disabling essential cookies through your browser settings may prevent you from logging in, completing orders, or accessing certain features of NEMEXIS. We recommend using our preference panel above instead of blocking all cookies at the browser level.
